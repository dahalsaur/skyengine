About us

SkyEngine is the group project for the course Databases and Web Services. We are designing online ticketing used by airlines by explaining the procedure and presenting a simple application.

The main focus of our project is to develop a working application. With the use of different applications and programs, a simple application identical to the models used by airlines companies today is developed. Research in the sources such as e-books, articles, documentation and tutorials have been used as references for the design and development of our project.
